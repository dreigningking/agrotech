ABOUT US

Green Farm Agro Technology Ltd

Green Farm Agro Technology Ltd is an agricultural production company that believes in the power of farming, processing, and packaging of local food products for both local consumption and export. We are committed to enhancing better yield, improving product quality, reducing post-harvest losses, and boosting agricultural development in our communities and beyond.

  • We enhance better yield through modern farming techniques and expert guidance.
  • We improve product qualities by adopting best practices in processing and packaging.
  • We reduce post-harvest losses with innovative storage and logistics solutions.
  • We boost agricultural development by empowering farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture.
Our Services

What We Offer

We provide a comprehensive range of agricultural services to support farmers, businesses, and communities.

Agricultural Consultancy

Expert advice and tailored solutions to help you maximize your farm's productivity and profitability.

Facilitate Precision Agricultural Practice

We introduce and support precision agriculture, leveraging technology for efficient resource use and higher yields.

Urban Farming Set Up & Promotion

We help individuals and organizations establish and promote urban farms, bringing fresh produce closer to city dwellers.

Commercial Organic Vegetable Production

We produce organic vegetables in commercial quantities for both local markets and export, ensuring quality and freshness.

Supply of Certified Hybrid/OP Seeds

We supply certified hybrid and open-pollinated seeds to guarantee high germination rates and robust crop performance.

Supply of Agricultural Produce & Products

We supply a vast range of agricultural produce and products, including grains, Gari (varieties and mixes), honey, coconut, and more, to meet the needs of both local and international clients.
